Elevating AI Security Every AI system you deploy is a potential attack surface. Models and agents can carry embedded backdoors, malicious operators or compromised dependencies. Once running, these artifacts can exfiltrate sensitive data or execute unauthorized code, creating persistent vulnerabilities within the enterprise perimeter. Organizations running AI workloads on Nutanix need security that catches these threats before they reach production.
