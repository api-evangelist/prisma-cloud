Palo Alto Networks announced the integration of Cortex XSIAM with NVIDIA DOCA Argus framework, enabling real-time AI-powered security operations within NVIDIA AI factories. The solution operates on NVIDIA BlueField processors to detect kernel-level threats and anomalies, and deploys Prisma AIRS to provide defense-in-depth security including AI Runtime Firewall protections against prompt injection and data leakage. The integration represents a partnership approach to secure AI infrastructure across the entire lifecycle, from data protection to autonomous agent operations.
